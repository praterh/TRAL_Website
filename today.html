Activities and Recreation

Ras Muhammad is known for its marine recreation, with Shark Reef and Yolanda Reef being popular scuba diving hotspots. Snorkeling and boat touring also provide opportunities for tourists to interact with the waters of the park. There are plenty of other actvities for the landlubbers who want to visit. While the park isn't known for it's hikes and trailing ventures, there are a few trails of varying difficulties to try out. There are also bird-watching, photography, and touring to engage with.
